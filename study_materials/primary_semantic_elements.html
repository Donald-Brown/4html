Primary Semantic HTML elements

  • header - The header for a page
  • main - The pages main content
  • section - a generic section that does not indicate type of content
  • article - A composition similar to a paper article
  • nav - A navigation section
  • aside - A section like a sidebar containing info about content near it
  • footer - The page footer
